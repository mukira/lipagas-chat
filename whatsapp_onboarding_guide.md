# LipaGas Chatwoot: WhatsApp Onboarding Guide

**Scenario:** You have a completely clean phone number (+254 143 043073) that has never been registered on the WhatsApp app, and you want to connect it to `chat.lipagas.com` to handle your customer support.

Follow these steps precisely:

---

## Phase 1: Meta Setup (Do this before touching Chatwoot)
Because WhatsApp is owned by Meta, you must have a Facebook presence to use their official API. 

1. **Create a Facebook Account:** 
   Go to [https://www.facebook.com/r.php](https://www.facebook.com/r.php) and create a standard personal account if you don't have one.
   
2. **Create a Business Page:** 
   Once logged in, go directly to the Page Creation portal: [https://www.facebook.com/pages/create/](https://www.facebook.com/pages/create/)
   Give it your business name (e.g., "XYZ Gas") and a category.
   *(Note: You do not need to post anything or build out this page, it just legally needs to exist for Meta).*

---

## Phase 2: Sign Up on LipaGas Chatwoot
1. **Create an Account:** 
   Go to [https://chat.lipagas.com/app/auth/signup](https://chat.lipagas.com/app/auth/signup) and create an account using your business email and a secure password.
   
2. **Navigate to Inboxes:** 
   Once you are logged into the dashboard, go to the bottom left and click **Settings**.
   
3. **Add an Inbox:** 
   Click on **Inboxes**, then click the **Add Inbox** button in the top right. 
   *(Or go directly here: [https://chat.lipagas.com/app/settings/inboxes/new](https://chat.lipagas.com/app/settings/inboxes/new))*
   
4. Click on the **WhatsApp** icon.

---

## Phase 3: Connecting Your Clean Number
1. On the WhatsApp setup screen, you will see a button that says **"Connect with WhatsApp Business"**. Click it.
2. A secure Meta popup will appear. Log in using the Facebook account you just created in Phase 1.
3. Click **Get Started** and select the Facebook Business Page you created.
4. Meta will ask you to create a "WhatsApp Business Profile". Enter your business name here.
5. **Add your phone number:** Enter your fresh number (**+254 143 043073**). 
6. Choose how you want to verify the number (SMS or Phone Call). 
7. Enter the 6-digit verification code you receive on that SIM card.
8. Click **Finish**.

**Done!**
The Meta popup will close, and Chatwoot will automatically create your WhatsApp inbox. Whenever a customer sends a message to +254 143 043073, it will immediately appear in your `chat.lipagas.com` dashboard!
