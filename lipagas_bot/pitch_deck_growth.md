### Option 1: The Step-by-Step Flow (Best for Pitch Decks)
**How LiPagas Works: The Frictionless Delivery Cycle**

1. **Seamless Ordering:** Customers place and pay for their LPG order directly through their preferred social media platforms, like WhatsApp—no new apps required.
2. **AI-Powered Open Bid:** Our AI instantly routes the order to the nearest verified vendors via a WhatsApp notification. Operating on an open-bid system, the first vendor to accept the request wins the delivery.
3. **Asset Verification:** Before handover, the vendor scans the cylinder’s QR code directly within WhatsApp, instantly authenticating the cylinder and confirming it is a legitimate, untampered asset.
4. **Instant Settlement:** Upon successful delivery, the vendor is paid out immediately, and the customer receives an automated e-receipt. Total transparency from start to finish.
