# Integrated Cylinder & Sales Tracking: The Path to Zero Loss

---

## 1. The Problem
Oil Marketing Companies (OMCs) face significant challenges in the downstream supply chain:
* **Cylinder Loss & Pilferage:** Millions are lost annually due to untraceable cylinders in circulation.
* **Lack of Visibility:** Once cylinders leave the bottling plant or main distributor, tracking them to the end consumer is a black box.
* **Inefficient Sales Tracking:** Disconnected systems make it difficult to reconcile sales data end-to-end.
* **High CapEx for New Systems:** Traditional IoT or tracking upgrades require massive infrastructure and hardware investments.

---

## 2. Our Solution
An integrated, end-to-end sales and cylinder tracking platform designed specifically for the LPG distribution ecosystem. 

**Our Promise: Track every cylinder to the last customer with ZERO cylinder loss.**

---

## 3. How It Works
We leverage intelligent software rather than expensive hardware to create a closed-loop tracking environment.

* **End-to-End Traceability:** A digital ledger that records every scan, transfer, and transaction from the bottling plant, to the distributor, to the delivery agent, and finally to the consumer.
* **Seamless Integration:** Plugs directly into your existing ERP and distributor management systems via APIs.
* **Data-Driven Reconciliation:** Automatically matches sales invoices with physical cylinder movements to identify anomalies in real-time.

## 5. Key Features
1. **Real-time Dashboard:** Global view of cylinder inventory across all distributors and transit routes.
2. **Delivery Agent App:** A simple mobile interface for delivery personnel to verify end-customer handover.
3. **Automated Alerts:** Instant notifications for delayed returns, unauthorized route deviations, or inventory mismatches.
4. **Consumer Verification:** OTP or QR-based digital handover confirmation to ensure the cylinder reached the right home.
